What We’ve Pulled Off

1. ML Models in Your Browser

Say goodbye to server-side lag—our machine learning models are moving straight into your browser with TensorFlow.js. No more back-and-forth calls; it’s all happening locally, saving time and keeping your data where it belongs: with you.

  • Model Conversion: We’ve converted our AI brains into TensorFlow.js-compatible formats (fancy .json and .bin files).
  • Real-Time Predictions: Now running in-browser scripts to serve up predictions on the fly.
  • Stress Tests: Compatibility runs on different browsers and devices to make sure it’s smooth sailing for everyone.

2. Data-Powered Everything

Data isn’t just fuel; it’s the whole damn engine. We’ve built an ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) pipeline that chews through data and spits out actionable insights.

  • Macroeconomic Trends: Interest rates, GDP, unemployment—you name it, we’re crunching it.
  • Alternative Data Sources: Social media buzz? Check. Parking lot traffic for retail stocks? Yep, even that.
  • Ready for Action: All this data is cleaned, structured, and ready to feed into our models.

3. Smarter Investment Suggestions

Our OpenAI integration has leveled up big time. Now, we’re blending macro trends, sentiment scores, and tech indicators to serve up real, actionable advice.

  • Better Prompts, Bigger Insights: Using richer data sources to generate suggestions that aren’t just smart—they’re tactical.
  • Deeper Analysis: Bollinger Bands, VWAP, and other advanced indicators are now part of the mix.
