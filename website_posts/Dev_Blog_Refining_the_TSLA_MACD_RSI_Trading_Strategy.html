Overview

Today’s Focus

Today, we refined a Tesla-specific trading strategy leveraging MACD and RSI indicators. The goal was to improve clarity, increase win rate, and document key insights for future maintainability.

1. Enhancing Clarity Through Comments and Documentation

  • Purpose of the Strategy: Explained the focus on identifying momentum shifts using MACD curls and RSI thresholds.
  • Key Metrics: Documented backtesting results, including a 63.64% win rate and a profit factor of 2.707.
  • Low-Quality Signals: Defined and filtered out signals during extreme volatility or indecisive RSI levels.

2. Adding Insights on Volatility Filtering

We introduced Bollinger Bands as a volatility filter, helping the strategy avoid trades during erratic market conditions. Bollinger Bands were chosen for their ability to define dynamic support/resistance levels.

3. Deep Dive Into ATR-Based Trailing Stops

  • Volatile Markets: Stops expand to avoid premature exits.
  • Stable Markets: Stops contract to secure gains effectively.

Backtesting showed that this approach improved profit factor and reduced drawdowns.

4. Preparing for Continuity

We ensured detailed comments and key metrics are documented to make the project easily resumable for future iterations.
