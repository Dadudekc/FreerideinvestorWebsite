Progress and Lessons—A Greedy Moment with TSLA

Hey FreeRiders,

The past couple of days have been a rollercoaster. While I’m still feeling the sting of some tough losses, I’ve also noticed progress in my trading approach—steps forward that I want to share alongside the lessons learned.

What Went Well

  • Spacing Out Entries: I made strides in spacing out my entries instead of going all-in on a single big order. By spreading out trades over two days, I gave myself more flexibility and time to evaluate the market.
  • Selling Losers Early: I took a hard but necessary step by selling some of my losers before they hit zero. Accepting a loss is never easy, but it’s part of becoming a disciplined trader.
  • Solid Positions: Some of the positions I’m holding are still looking solid, and I’m optimistic about their potential.

Where I Went Wrong

When Tesla rocketed right off the open, I let excitement get the best of me. Instead of seeing it as an opportunity to lock in gains or reassess, I took the rally as confirmation and rushed to get in further. That moment of greed—thinking I’d catch a bigger move—has left me in the position I’m in now.

The Lesson

Confirmation isn’t always what it seems. A sharp move can be just as much a warning as it is an opportunity, and this experience reminds me to stick to the plan rather than chase the dream in the moment.

What’s Next

  • Refinement Over Perfection: These are the growing pains of improving as a trader. Each day, I’m tweaking my approach, and even small wins—like accepting losses earlier—matter.
  • Patience and Discipline: I’ll be focusing on refining my exit strategies and practicing more self-control when the market gets volatile.
  • Transparency: As always, I’ll share the outcomes, good or bad, because this journey is about learning and growing together.

Trading isn’t a straight path to success; it’s a constant balancing act between progress and setbacks. I’m proud of the improvements I’ve made, and I’ll carry the lessons from these trades forward.

Thank you all for being here through the ups and downs. Let’s keep learning and building together.

Conclusion

By continuously refining our strategies, learning from our mistakes, and supporting each other, we can achieve remarkable success in the financial markets. Stay tuned for more insights, strategies, and updates as we continue this journey together.
