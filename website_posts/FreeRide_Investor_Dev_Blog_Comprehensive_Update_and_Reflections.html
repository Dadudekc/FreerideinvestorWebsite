Personal Reflections: A Lesson in Discipline

Today was a lesson.

I had a plan—a solid plan. I knew my levels, my triggers, my stops. But when the trade started moving, greed stepped in. I let a $400 profit almost turn into a $900 loss before walking away with just $27.

  • Have a plan and trust it.
  • Take the small wins—they compound over time.
  • Stick to my stops—protect my account first.
  • Stay in control. I trade the plan, not my emotions.
